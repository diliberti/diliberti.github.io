Supervision

I supervise Master and PhD students in the macro-topics below. Click here for a detailed list of possible thesis projects.

  • Categorical Logic and Logic.

  • Category theory and Formal Category Theory.

  • Categorical semantics of Type Theory.

  • Interactions between Logic and Geometry.

  • Categorical approaches to Geometry and Topology.

If you do not like any of the projects in the list or you have some topic to suggest, feel free to contact me with any idea of yours, we will evaluate it together. .

I am willing to supervise students that are not based at Gothenburg University. Send me an email. In order to make my life easier, make sure to include the word THSUP in the subject.
